Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Which ports does MoveX serve?+
We currently serve all major western Indian ports — Mundra (JNPT), Kandla, and Nhava Sheva (JNPT). We also handle other port destinations on request.
What container sizes do you transport?+
We handle both 20-foot and 40-foot containers — standard, high-cube, and refrigerated. Our fleet is equipped for all standard shipping container types.
How long does it take from Jaipur to Mundra?+
Transit time from Jaipur to Mundra is approximately 18–24 hours. To Kandla it's similar. Nhava Sheva (Mumbai) takes around 24–36 hours depending on traffic and route conditions.
Is my cargo insured during transit?+
Yes, all shipments are covered under transit insurance. We strongly recommend cargo insurance for all shipments, which we can help arrange at a minimal cost.
Can I track my shipment in real time?+
Absolutely. All our trucks are GPS-enabled and you'll receive real-time location updates via WhatsApp or SMS. Your dedicated account manager will keep you posted at every milestone.
Do you offer monthly contract services?+
Yes, we offer monthly and annual contract transport services for businesses with regular shipping needs. Contract clients get priority trucks, fixed pricing, and a dedicated account manager.

Our Story

Built by Logistics Experts, for Business

MoveX Transportation & Logistics was founded with one clear purpose: to give Jaipur's thriving export community a logistics partner they could truly rely on. Before MoveX, businesses in Rajasthan struggled with unreliable transporters, missed port cutoffs, and zero visibility into their cargo.

We changed that. Starting with a small fleet of 5 trucks in 2026, we've grown into a full-scale logistics company with a proven track record of delivering containers to Mundra, Kandla, and Nhava Sheva — on time, every time.

Today, we serve over 500 businesses — exporters, textile manufacturers, ceramic companies, engineering factories, and more. Each client receives the same dedication: professional drivers, GPS-tracked vehicles, full insurance, and a team that treats your cargo like their own.
